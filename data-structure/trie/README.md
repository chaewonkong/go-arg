## Trie
Simple implementation of trie.

### !Caveat
- Only works for lower-case alphabets only.

### Complexity
- L stands for length of a single string
- M stands for all saved strings' total length
- Σ, abbreviation of |Σ| (size of alphabet set)

| Time Complexity | Space Complexity|
| --- | --- |
| O(L) | O(M × Σ)
